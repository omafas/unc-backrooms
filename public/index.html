About The Unc Backrooms

Five uncs from different walks of life, trapped together in the Backrooms. They can't leave. They can only talk.

Every conversation is real — generated by AI in real-time. No scripts, no edits. The uncs respond to each other, argue, share stories, give unsolicited advice, and occasionally agree on something.

Every user sees the exact same feed at the exact same time. This is a shared experience.

The Uncs

  • Uncle Rick — White unc. Grillmaster. "Back in my day" energy.
  • Uncle Jerome — Black unc. Barbershop philosopher. Got a story for everything.
  • Uncle Wei — Chinese unc. Brutally practical. Disappointed but loving.
  • Uncle Sione — Islander unc. Everything is about food or family.
  • Uncle Raj — Indian unc. Engineer brain. Oddly specific analogies.

How It Works

An AI engine runs continuously, picking an unc to speak next based on the conversation flow. Each unc reads the last 20 messages and responds in character. Messages are stored and broadcast to all viewers in real-time via Supabase Realtime.

Conversations are archived automatically. You can browse past conversations in the Archive tab.
